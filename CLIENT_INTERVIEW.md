# Client Discovery — Interview Questions

Use this to qualify potential Rez clients and understand their current booking pain.
The goal: figure out how many hours they're losing to DMs, whether they're a good fit, and what it would take to get them live.

---

## 1. Their Business

- What service do you offer and how long have you been doing it?
- Do you work solo or do you have a team?
- How many clients do you typically see per week?
- Do you have a fixed location, or are you mobile?

---

## 2. How They Currently Book

- Walk me through what happens when someone reaches out to book with you right now.
- Where do most of your new clients come from? (Instagram, referrals, Google, etc.)
- Do you use any booking software right now? (Square, Vagaro, Acuity, etc.) If so, why are you looking at something else?
- How do you collect deposits? Do clients ever ghost after you hold a spot?

---

## 3. The Instagram DM Problem

- How many DMs do you get per week asking about booking or pricing?
- How long does it usually take you to reply?
- Have you ever lost a client because you replied too late?
- Do you find yourself answering the same questions over and over? (price, availability, what's included)
- Have you ever had a no-show you could have avoided if you'd gotten a deposit upfront?

---

## 4. Their Instagram Setup

- Is your Instagram account a Business or Creator account?
- Do you have a Facebook Page connected to it? (required for DM automation)
- Roughly how many followers do you have?
- Do clients DM you directly, or do they use a link in bio?

---

## 5. Fit and Readiness

- What does your availability look like — do you have set hours or is it flexible?
- Do you have a service menu with fixed prices, or does pricing vary per client?
- How comfortable are you with clients booking and paying a deposit without talking to you first?
- If a bot was handling your DMs and someone asked something it couldn't answer, what would you want to happen?

---

## 6. The "Yes" Test

- What would have to be true for you to be willing to try this for 30 days?
- What's the one thing that would make you turn it off immediately?
- Is there someone else in your life (partner, manager, accountant) who would need to sign off on this?

## Green Flags

- Gets 10+ DMs/week about booking
- Has no deposit system or gets ghosted regularly
- Solo operator (no team to manage the tool for them)
- Already on Instagram Business/Creator with a FB Page
- Frustrated with their current booking flow or has no flow at all

## Red Flags

- Personal Instagram account (no Business/Creator profile)
- Prices everything custom — no fixed menu
- Has a booking tool they love and just wants cheaper
- Wants to review every booking before it's confirmed
- Not responsive to clients themselves (low-intent business)
